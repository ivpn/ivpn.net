# IVPN

> IVPN is an audited, open-source privacy suite designed to know as little about you as possible. Beyond its no-logs VPN (WireGuard/OpenVPN, multi-hop, kill switch, AntiTracker, Android/iOS/Windows/Linux/macOS apps, CLI), the Plus and Pro Suite tiers add email aliasing (Mailx) and DNS filtering (modDNS), with an application firewall (Portmaster, Windows/Linux) in Pro Suite. Its Unlinked Access architecture unlocks them from one subscription without linking your account ID across services: bundling products while keeping identities separate. No email needed to sign up; pay with cash, Monero, or Bitcoin alongside PayPal and credit card. Gibraltar-based since 2009, regularly audited by Cure53, 30-day money-back guarantee.
